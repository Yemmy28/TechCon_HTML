History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the goal of bringing together technology enthusiasts and professionals from around the world. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential tech conferences globally.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and connect the tech community by showcasing the latest advancements and fostering innovation. We believe in the power of technology to transform the world.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted many notable speakers who have made significant contributions to the tech industry.

Goodnews Edeth

Goodnews Edeth is a renowned software engineer known for his work on artificial intelligence. He spoke at TechCon 2020 about the future of AI.

Eline Promise

Eline Promise is a cybersecurity expert who has helped shape modern security protocols. Her talk at TechCon 2021 focused on the importance of cybersecurity in the digital age.

Michael Johnson

Michael Johnson is a pioneer in blockchain technology. His presentation at TechCon 2022 delved into the potential of blockchain to revolutionize various industries.
